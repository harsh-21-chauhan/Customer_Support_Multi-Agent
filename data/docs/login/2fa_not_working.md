# 2FA not working

## Category
login

## Problem
You are unable to log in to your account because Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) is not working, preventing you from accessing the application. 

## Solution
1. Ensure that your phone or authentication app is receiving the 2FA codes correctly. Check your phone's signal strength and try restarting your phone if necessary.
2. Verify that the time on your phone or authentication app is synchronized with the current time, as a time discrepancy can cause 2FA codes to be invalid.
3. Check that you are entering the 2FA code correctly, paying close attention to the code's format and any leading or trailing zeros.
4. If using an authentication app, ensure it is configured correctly for our application and that the secret key was entered accurately during setup.
5. Try resetting your 2FA setup by going to your account settings, disabling 2FA, and then re-enabling it to generate a new secret key.
6. If issues persist, try using a different method for 2FA, such as switching from an authentication app to SMS codes or vice versa.


## Notes
- If you have recently changed phones or authentication apps, you may need to reconfigure 2FA. - Some users may experience issues with 2FA due to browser or device caching; trying a different browser or clearing your cache may resolve the issue. - In rare cases, 2FA may not work due to technical issues on our side; if none of the above steps resolve the issue, contact our support team for further assistance.
