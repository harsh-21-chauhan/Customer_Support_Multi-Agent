# Login after password change failed

## Category
login

## Problem
You are unable to log in to your account after changing your password, and you are receiving an error message indicating that your credentials are incorrect. 

## Solution
1. Ensure that you are using the new password you created, as the old password will no longer be valid.
2. Double-check that you are entering the correct username and new password, paying close attention to uppercase and lowercase letters, as passwords are case-sensitive.
3. Try clearing your browser's cache and cookies, then attempt to log in again.
4. If you are using a password manager, verify that it has been updated with your new password.
5. If you are still having trouble, try resetting your password again to ensure it was changed correctly.


## Notes
If you have two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled on your account, you may need to complete the 2FA process after entering your new password. Additionally, if you have multiple accounts with the same username, ensure you are attempting to log in to the correct account. If you continue to experience issues, you may want to try logging in from a different browser or device to rule out any browser-specific or device-specific problems.
