# Account suspended message

## Category
login

## Problem
You are receiving an "Account Suspended" message when trying to log in to your account, and you are unable to access the application. 

## Solution
1. Check your email for a suspension notice from our support team, which may include the reason for the suspension and any required actions to resolve the issue.
2. Review your account status by contacting our support team directly to understand the cause of the suspension.
3. If your account was suspended due to non-payment, update your payment information or settle any outstanding invoices to reactivate your account.
4. If your account was suspended due to a security issue or terms of service violation, follow the instructions provided by our support team to resolve the issue and reactivate your account.
5. Once the issue is resolved, your account will be reactivated, and you will receive a confirmation email.


## Notes
Account suspensions can occur due to various reasons, including non-payment, security concerns, or terms of service violations. In some cases, accounts may be suspended temporarily or permanently, depending on the severity of the issue. If you are unsure about the reason for the suspension or the required actions to resolve the issue, please contact our support team for assistance.
