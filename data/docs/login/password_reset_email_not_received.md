# Password reset email not received

## Category
login

## Problem
You are trying to reset your password, but you are not receiving the password reset email. 

## Solution
1. Check your spam or junk mail folder to ensure the email was not filtered out.
2. Verify that you are using the correct email address associated with your account.
3. Try resending the password reset email from the login page.
4. If you have a secondary email address associated with your account, try using that email address to reset your password.
5. If you are still having trouble, try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then attempt to reset your password again.


## Notes
If you have recently updated your email address, it may take a few minutes for the change to propagate. Additionally, some email providers may have stricter spam filters, which could delay or block the password reset email. If you are using a work or school email address, your organization's email policies may be blocking the email.
