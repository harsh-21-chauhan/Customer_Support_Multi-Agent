# Account locked after failed attempts

## Category
login

## Problem
Your account is locked after multiple failed login attempts, preventing you from accessing the application. 

## Solution
1. Wait for 30 minutes to allow the temporary lock to expire, after which you can try logging in again.
2. If you are still having trouble, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.
3. Enter your email address associated with your account and follow the password reset instructions sent to you via email.
4. If you are unable to reset your password, contact our support team using the "Contact Support" link at the bottom of the login page.
5. Provide your account email address and a detailed description of the issue, and our support team will assist you in unlocking your account.


## Notes
Account locks are in place to protect your account from unauthorized access. If you are experiencing repeated lockouts, ensure that you are using the correct email address and password. Additionally, be cautious when using password managers or auto-fill features, as they may cause incorrect login attempts. If you have recently changed your password, ensure that all devices and browsers are updated with the new credentials.
