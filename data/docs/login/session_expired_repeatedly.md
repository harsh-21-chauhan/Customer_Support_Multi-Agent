# Session expired repeatedly

## Category
login

## Problem
You are being logged out of your account repeatedly, with the system displaying a "Session expired" message, even after a short period of inactivity. 

## Solution
1. Check your browser settings to ensure that cookies are enabled, as our application relies on cookies to maintain your session.
2. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try logging in again to see if the issue persists.
3. If you are using a browser extension that blocks or manages cookies, try disabling it or adding our site to its whitelist.
4. Ensure that your browser is up-to-date, as older versions may have compatibility issues with our application.
5. If you are accessing our application from a public or shared computer, try logging in from a private computer to rule out any issues with the shared environment.


## Notes
If you are using a strict cookie policy or have a firewall that blocks certain types of cookies, you may experience session expiration issues. Additionally, if you are using a mobile device, try closing and reopening the browser or app to see if the issue resolves. In some cases, a simple restart of your device may also resolve the issue.
