# Login from new device blocked

## Category
login

## Problem
You are trying to log in to your account from a new device, but the system is blocking your attempt and requiring additional verification. 

## Solution
1. Check your email for a verification message sent by our system, which should arrive within a few minutes of your login attempt.
2. Click on the verification link provided in the email to confirm that the login attempt is legitimate.
3. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or ensure that emails from our domain are not being blocked by your email provider.
4. Once you have verified your identity, you should be able to log in to your account from the new device.
5. If you are still having trouble, try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then attempt to log in again.


## Notes
This additional verification step is in place to protect your account from unauthorized access. If you are logging in from a new device frequently (e.g., due to travel or working from multiple locations), you may want to consider using an authenticator app or a VPN to reduce the likelihood of being blocked. In some cases, certain network configurations or browser extensions may interfere with the verification process.
