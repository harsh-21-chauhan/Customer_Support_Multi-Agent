# IP blocked during login

## Category
login

## Problem
You are unable to log in to your account because your IP address has been blocked due to multiple failed login attempts or suspicious activity. 

## Solution
1. Wait for 30 minutes to allow the temporary block to expire, after which you can try logging in again.
2. If you are still having issues, try resetting your password to ensure you are using the correct credentials.
3. If you are using a VPN or proxy, disable it and try logging in again, as these services can sometimes trigger our security measures.
4. If none of the above steps work, contact our support team with your account details and the error message you are seeing, and we will investigate the issue further.
5. To prevent future blocks, ensure that you and any other users of your account are using the correct login credentials and avoid attempting to log in multiple times with incorrect information.


## Notes
Please note that repeated instances of failed login attempts from the same IP address may result in a longer block period. Additionally, if our system detects what appears to be a brute-force attack or other malicious activity, the block may be permanent and require manual review by our support team to resolve. In some cases, certain network configurations or ISP issues may also trigger our security measures, so it's a good idea to try logging in from a different network if possible.
