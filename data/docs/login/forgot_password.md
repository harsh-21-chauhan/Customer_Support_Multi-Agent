# Forgot password

## Category
login

## Problem
You are unable to log in to your account because you have forgotten your password. 

## Solution
1. Click on the "Forgot Password" link located on the login page.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account in the provided field.
3. Click the "Reset Password" button to send a password reset email to your email address.
4. Check your email inbox for the password reset email and click on the "Reset Password" link.
5. Enter a new password and confirm it by re-entering it in the required field.
6. Click the "Save" button to update your password.


## Notes
If you do not receive the password reset email, check your spam or junk mail folder. If you are still unable to find the email, ensure that your email address is correctly associated with your account. Additionally, password reset links are only valid for a limited time, so if the link has expired, you will need to request a new password reset.
