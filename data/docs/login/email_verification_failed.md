# Email verification failed

## Category
login

## Problem
You are unable to log in to your account because the email verification process has failed, preventing you from accessing the platform. 

## Solution
1. Check your email inbox for the verification email sent by our system, ensuring it hasn't been marked as spam or junk.
2. If you can't find the email, try resending the verification email by clicking on the "Resend Verification Email" link on the login page.
3. Verify that the email address you are trying to verify is correctly spelled and matches the one associated with your account.
4. Click on the verification link in the email to complete the verification process.
5. If you are still having issues, try clearing your browser cache and cookies before attempting to verify your email again.


## Notes
If your email provider has strict spam filters, it may block our verification emails. In such cases, try checking your spam folder or contacting your email provider to whitelist our domain. Additionally, if you have previously verified your email address and are still experiencing issues, it's possible that your account has been flagged for security reasons, requiring you to contact our support team for assistance.
