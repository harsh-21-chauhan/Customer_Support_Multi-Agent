# SSO login failed

## Category
login

## Problem
You are unable to log in to our application using Single Sign-On (SSO) and are receiving an error message. 

## Solution
1. Ensure that your organization has configured SSO correctly in our application's settings.
2. Verify that you are using the correct SSO login URL provided by your organization or our support team.
3. Check that your SSO credentials are up-to-date and match the ones used for other SSO-enabled applications.
4. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try logging in again.
5. If you are still experiencing issues, contact your organization's IT department to confirm that SSO is properly set up on their end.


## Notes
If your organization uses a custom SSO configuration, you may need to provide additional information to our support team to troubleshoot the issue. Additionally, some SSO providers may have specific requirements or restrictions that can affect login functionality. In rare cases, SSO login failures can be caused by network or server-side issues, which our support team can help investigate.
