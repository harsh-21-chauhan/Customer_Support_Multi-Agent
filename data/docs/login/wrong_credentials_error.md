# Wrong credentials error

## Category
login

## Problem
You are receiving a "wrong credentials" error when attempting to log in to your account, despite entering what you believe to be the correct username and password. 

## Solution
1. Double-check that you are using the correct username and password combination. Make sure to check for any typos or extra spaces in your username or password.
2. If you have forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to initiate a password reset.
3. Ensure that your password meets the minimum requirements (at least 8 characters, including at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number).
4. If you are copying and pasting your password, try typing it in manually instead, as some characters may not be copied correctly.
5. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try logging in again to ensure you are not experiencing any issues related to stored browser data.


## Notes
If you have recently changed your password, it may take a few minutes for the change to propagate to all systems. Additionally, if you are using a password manager to generate and store complex passwords, ensure that it is configured correctly and not introducing any incorrect characters. If you continue to experience issues after trying the above steps, you may want to try logging in from a different browser or device to rule out any browser-specific or device-specific problems.
