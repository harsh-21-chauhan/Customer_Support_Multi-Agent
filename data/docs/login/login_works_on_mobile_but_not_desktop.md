# Login works on mobile but not desktop

## Category
login

## Problem
You are able to log in to our application on your mobile device, but you are unable to log in on your desktop computer, despite using the same credentials. 

## Solution
1. Ensure that you are using the correct username and password on both devices. Double-check that your caps lock key is not enabled and that you are entering the correct characters.
2. Clear your browser's cache and cookies on your desktop computer. This can resolve issues related to stored login information or corrupted data.
3. Try using a different web browser on your desktop computer to rule out any browser-specific issues.
4. Check that your desktop computer's date and time settings are accurate, as an incorrect date or time can cause login issues.
5. If you are using a VPN or proxy server on your desktop computer, try disabling it and attempting to log in again.


## Notes
If you are using a company-managed desktop computer, your IT department may have implemented security policies that are blocking our application's login functionality. Additionally, if you have recently changed your password, it may take a few minutes for the change to propagate to all of our servers. In some cases, firewall settings or antivirus software may also interfere with the login process.
