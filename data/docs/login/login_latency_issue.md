# Login latency issue

## Category
login

## Problem
Users are experiencing delays when attempting to log in to their accounts, resulting in a slow loading time for the dashboard. 

## Solution
1. Check your internet connection to ensure it is stable and working properly.
2. Clear your browser's cache and cookies to remove any outdated or corrupted data that may be causing the issue.
3. Try logging in using a different browser or device to isolate the problem.
4. If you are using a VPN, try disabling it and log in again to see if it resolves the issue.
5. If none of the above steps work, try checking the system status page for any ongoing maintenance or outages that may be affecting login times.


## Notes
Login latency can also be caused by high traffic volumes or server maintenance. If you are experiencing persistent issues, it's possible that there may be a temporary overload on our servers. In such cases, waiting for a few minutes and trying again may resolve the issue. Additionally, ad blockers or certain browser extensions may interfere with the login process, so try disabling them if you suspect they may be the cause.
