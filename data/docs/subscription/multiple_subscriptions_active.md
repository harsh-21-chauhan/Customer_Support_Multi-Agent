# Multiple subscriptions active

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You have multiple active subscriptions for the same service, and you want to manage or cancel the duplicate subscriptions to avoid unnecessary charges. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Subscriptions" or "Billing" section.
2. Review your active subscriptions and identify the duplicates.
3. Click on the "Cancel" or "Delete" button next to each duplicate subscription to terminate them.
4. Confirm that you want to cancel the selected subscriptions.
5. Verify that only one active subscription remains for the service.


## Notes
If you have already been charged for a duplicate subscription, you may need to contact support to request a refund. Additionally, canceling a subscription will not automatically refund previous payments. Be cautious when canceling subscriptions, as this may affect access to certain features or services.
