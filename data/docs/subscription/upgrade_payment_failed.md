# Upgrade payment failed

## Category
subscription

## Problem
Your payment method failed when attempting to upgrade your subscription, resulting in an incomplete upgrade process. 

## Solution
1. Check your payment method: Ensure that your credit card information is up-to-date and that the card has not expired.
2. Verify available funds: Confirm that you have sufficient funds in your account to cover the upgrade cost.
3. Try an alternative payment method: If possible, attempt to use a different credit card or payment method to complete the upgrade.
4. Contact your bank: Reach out to your bank to see if there are any restrictions or issues on your account that may be causing the payment failure.
5. Retry the upgrade: Once you have resolved any issues with your payment method, attempt to upgrade your subscription again.


## Notes
If you are using a prepaid card or a card with limited international transactions, it may not be compatible with our payment system. Additionally, some banks may flag our payment requests as suspicious, so it's essential to contact them to verify the transaction. If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team for further assistance.
