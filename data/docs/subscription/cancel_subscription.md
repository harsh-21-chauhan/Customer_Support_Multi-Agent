# Cancel subscription

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You want to cancel your subscription to our SaaS product, but you're not sure how to do it or what the implications will be. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account on our website.
2. Click on your profile picture or username in the top right corner, then select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu.
3. Scroll down to the "Subscription" section.
4. Click on the "Cancel Subscription" button.
5. Confirm that you want to cancel your subscription by clicking "Yes, Cancel Subscription" in the popup window.
6. You will receive an email confirming that your subscription has been cancelled.


## Notes
Cancelling your subscription will immediately stop any future billing, but it will not provide a refund for any previous payments. You will still have access to our product until the end of your current billing cycle. If you have any outstanding invoices, you will still be required to pay them. If you cancel your subscription by mistake, you can contact our support team to have it reinstated, but this must be done before the end of your current billing cycle.
