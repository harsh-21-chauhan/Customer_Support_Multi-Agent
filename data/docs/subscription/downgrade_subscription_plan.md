# Downgrade subscription plan

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You want to downgrade your current subscription plan to a lower-tier plan, but you're not sure how to do it or what the implications will be. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Subscription" or "Billing" section, usually found in the settings or profile area.
2. Click on the "Change Plan" or "Upgrade/Downgrade" button, which may be located near your current plan details.
3. Select the lower-tier plan you wish to downgrade to from the available options.
4. Review the changes to your plan, including any changes to features, limits, or pricing.
5. Confirm that you want to downgrade your plan, acknowledging any potential loss of features or functionality.
6. Your plan will be updated immediately, and any changes to your billing will be reflected in your next invoice.


## Notes
Downgrading your plan may result in the loss of certain features or functionality, so review the plan details carefully before making a change. If you're downgrading due to billing concerns, consider contacting our support team to discuss potential alternatives. Additionally, some features or data may not be retained when downgrading, so it's essential to review the plan details and our documentation before making a change.
