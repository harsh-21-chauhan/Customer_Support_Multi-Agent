# Subscription not visible

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You have purchased a subscription to our SaaS product, but it is not visible in your account dashboard. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the account dashboard.
2. Click on the "Subscriptions" tab to view a list of all active and inactive subscriptions associated with your account.
3. If you do not see your subscription listed, check your email for a confirmation email with a link to activate your subscription.
4. If you have already activated your subscription, try refreshing the page or logging out and logging back in to sync your account information.
5. If your subscription is still not visible after trying the above steps, contact our support team with your order number and a detailed description of the issue.


## Notes
If you have multiple accounts with our SaaS product, ensure that you are logged in to the correct account. Additionally, if you have purchased a subscription through a third-party reseller, it may take up to 24 hours for the subscription to be reflected in your account dashboard.
