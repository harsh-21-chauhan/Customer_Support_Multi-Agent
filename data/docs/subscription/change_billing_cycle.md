# Change billing cycle

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You want to change the billing cycle for your subscription, but you're not sure how to do it or what options are available. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Subscription" or "Billing" section, depending on your account layout.
2. Click on the "Edit" or "Update" button next to your current billing cycle.
3. Select your desired billing cycle from the available options (e.g., monthly, quarterly, annually).
4. Review and confirm the changes to your billing cycle, including any updates to your payment schedule or amount due.
5. Save your changes to update your billing cycle.


## Notes
Changing your billing cycle may affect your payment due dates and the amount you are charged. If you are switching to a longer billing cycle (e.g., from monthly to annually), you will be charged the new amount immediately, and your next payment due date will be updated accordingly. If you are switching to a shorter billing cycle, your next payment due date will be updated, but you will not receive a refund for any remaining time on your current cycle.
