# Re-activate canceled plan

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You have canceled your subscription plan, but you want to re-activate it and continue using the service without losing your existing data and settings. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account on our website using your email address and password.
2. Click on your profile picture or username in the top right corner, then select "Account" or "Subscription" from the dropdown menu.
3. Locate the "Canceled subscriptions" or "Inactive plans" section, and find the plan you want to re-activate.
4. Click on the "Re-activate" or "Restore" button next to the canceled plan.
5. Review the plan details, including the pricing and features, to ensure it's the correct one.
6. Confirm that you want to re-activate the plan by clicking on the "Re-activate" or "Confirm" button.
7. Your plan will be re-activated, and you will receive a confirmation email with the updated subscription details.


## Notes
Re-activating a canceled plan will resume your subscription from the date of re-activation, and you will be charged the current pricing for the plan. If you had any custom settings or configurations, they will be preserved. However, if you have downgraded or changed your plan since canceling, you may lose access to some features or data. Additionally, if your account has been inactive for an extended period, some data may have been archived or deleted due to inactivity.
