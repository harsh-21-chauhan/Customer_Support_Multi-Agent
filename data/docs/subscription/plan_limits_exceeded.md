# Plan limits exceeded

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You are receiving an error message indicating that your current plan limits have been exceeded, and you are unsure of how to resolve the issue or upgrade your plan. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Subscription" or "Billing" section.
2. Review your current plan details, including the limits for users, storage, and any other relevant features.
3. Identify which specific limit you have exceeded (e.g., number of users, storage capacity).
4. Click on the "Upgrade Plan" button to view available plan options that meet your needs.
5. Select a new plan that accommodates your requirements, and follow the prompts to complete the upgrade process.
6. Review and agree to the updated terms and pricing.
7. Confirm your plan change, and wait for the update to take effect.


## Notes
If you are nearing your plan limits, consider archiving or deleting unused data to free up space. Some features may not be available immediately after upgrading, as they may require additional setup or configuration. Downgrading your plan may result in loss of access to certain features or data, so please review the changes carefully before making any adjustments. Contact support if you need assistance with custom plan options or have questions about the upgrade process.
