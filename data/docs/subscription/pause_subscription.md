# Pause subscription

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You want to temporarily stop your subscription without canceling it, but you're not sure how to pause it in our system. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Subscription" or "Billing" section, depending on your account type.
2. Click on the "Manage Subscription" or "Edit Subscription" button.
3. Look for the "Pause Subscription" option and click on it.
4. Select the duration for which you want to pause your subscription, if available.
5. Confirm that you want to pause your subscription by clicking on the "Pause" button.
6. You will receive a confirmation email once your subscription has been paused.


## Notes
Pausing your subscription will not refund any previous payments, and you will not be able to access premium features during the paused period. When you pause your subscription, any recurring payments will be suspended until you resume it. Please note that some features may not be available or may be limited during the paused period. If you have any outstanding invoices, pausing your subscription will not cancel them.
