# Subscription renewal failed

## Category
subscription

## Problem
Your subscription renewal has failed, and you are unable to access your account or receive an error message indicating that the renewal was unsuccessful. 

## Solution
1. Check your payment method: Ensure that your credit card information is up to date and that the card has not expired.
2. Verify your billing address: Confirm that your billing address matches the address associated with your credit card.
3. Check for any declined payments: Review your payment history to see if any recent payments have been declined.
4. Attempt a manual renewal: Try renewing your subscription manually by logging into your account and navigating to the subscription settings page.
5. Contact your bank: If you are still experiencing issues, reach out to your bank to see if there are any restrictions or blocks on your account that may be preventing the renewal.


## Notes
If you are using a prepaid credit card or a card with limited international transaction capabilities, you may experience issues with renewal. Additionally, some banks may flag recurring payments as suspicious, so it's a good idea to notify your bank in advance of the renewal date. If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team for further assistance.
