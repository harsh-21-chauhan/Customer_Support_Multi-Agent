# Downgrade effective date confusion

## Category
subscription

## Problem
When downgrading a subscription plan, the effective date of the change is unclear, causing confusion about when the new plan will take effect and how it will impact billing. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the subscription management page.
2. Click on the "Change Plan" button and select the desired lower-tier plan.
3. Review the confirmation page, which will display the effective date of the downgrade.
4. The effective date will typically be the start of the next billing cycle, but may be immediate if you are downgrading mid-cycle and have already paid for the current cycle.
5. Verify that the new plan details and pricing are correct, and confirm the change.
6. Check your email for a confirmation notification, which will also include the effective date of the downgrade.


## Notes
If you have any outstanding invoices or credits, the downgrade may not take effect immediately. In such cases, the effective date will be delayed until the outstanding balance is settled. Additionally, downgrading a plan may result in the loss of certain features or functionality, so it's essential to review the plan details carefully before confirming the change.
