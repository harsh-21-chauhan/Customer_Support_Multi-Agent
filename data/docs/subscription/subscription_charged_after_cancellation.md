# Subscription charged after cancellation

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You have cancelled your subscription, but you have still been charged for the next billing period. 

## Solution
1. Check your email for a cancellation confirmation email from our system to ensure that your cancellation was successfully processed.
2. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section to verify the status of your subscription.
3. If your subscription shows as cancelled, but you have still been charged, please contact our support team with your account details and the date of cancellation.
4. Our support team will investigate the issue and refund any incorrect charges as soon as possible.
5. To avoid any further charges, we will also verify that your subscription has been properly removed from our billing system.


## Notes
In some cases, it may take a few days for the cancellation to be fully processed and reflected in our system. If you cancelled your subscription close to the billing date, you may still be charged for the current period, but you will not be charged again in the future. Refunds for incorrect charges will be processed within 5-7 business days.
