# Trial expired

## Category
subscription

## Problem
Your trial period has expired and you are no longer able to access the full features of our SaaS product. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account on our website to view your subscription status.
2. Click on the "Upgrade" or "Subscribe" button to select a paid plan that suits your needs.
3. Review and agree to the terms of service and pricing details.
4. Enter your payment information to complete the subscription process.
5. Once the payment is processed, your account will be upgraded to a paid subscription, and you will regain access to the full features of our product.


## Notes
If you were using a credit card to pay for your subscription and it has expired, you will need to update your payment information before you can reactivate your account. Additionally, if you have any outstanding invoices or unpaid balances, you will need to settle these before you can upgrade or reactivate your subscription. Please note that once a trial period has expired, any data stored in your trial account will be retained for a limited time (typically 30 days) before being deleted, so it's essential to upgrade or export your data promptly.
