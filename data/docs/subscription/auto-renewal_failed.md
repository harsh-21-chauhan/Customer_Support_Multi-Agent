# Auto-renewal failed

## Category
subscription

## Problem
Your subscription's auto-renewal has failed, resulting in an expired or suspended account status. 

## Solution
1. Check your payment method: Ensure that your credit card information is up-to-date and that the card has not expired.
2. Verify your account balance: Confirm that you have sufficient funds in your account to cover the renewal charge.
3. Review your subscription settings: Log in to your account and navigate to the subscription settings page to confirm that auto-renewal is enabled.
4. Attempt a manual renewal: Try manually renewing your subscription through the subscription settings page.
5. Contact your bank: If you have confirmed that your payment method and account balance are valid, contact your bank to check for any potential issues with the transaction.


## Notes
If your auto-renewal fails due to a declined payment, you will have a 24-hour grace period to update your payment method or make a manual payment before your account is suspended. Additionally, if you are using a prepaid card or a card with limited international transaction capabilities, you may experience issues with auto-renewal. In such cases, consider using an alternative payment method.
