# Upgrade subscription plan

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You want to upgrade your current subscription plan to a higher tier with more features and storage, but you're not sure how to do it within the application. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Settings" or "Account" section, depending on your application's layout.
2. Click on the "Subscription" or "Plan" tab to view your current plan details.
3. Click the "Upgrade" button next to your current plan to view available higher-tier plans.
4. Select the new plan you want to upgrade to and review the pricing and features.
5. Confirm your upgrade by clicking the "Upgrade Now" button and completing any additional payment information if required.
6. Wait for the upgrade to process, which may take a few minutes, and then verify that your new plan is active and features are available.


## Notes
When upgrading, any changes to your subscription will take effect immediately, and you will be charged the new plan's rate on your next billing cycle. If you're upgrading from a free trial or a promotional plan, your new plan's pricing will be based on the standard rates. Downgrading your plan is also possible, but it may result in loss of features or data, so it's essential to review the changes before confirming.
