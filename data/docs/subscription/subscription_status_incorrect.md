# Subscription status incorrect

## Category
subscription

## Problem
Your subscription status is not reflecting the correct information, such as showing as inactive when you have paid or showing as active when you have cancelled. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the subscription management page.
2. Check the payment history to ensure that your latest payment has been processed successfully.
3. If your payment has been processed, click on the "Refresh Status" button to update your subscription status.
4. If the issue persists, try logging out and logging back in to your account to refresh the page.
5. If none of the above steps resolve the issue, contact our support team with a screenshot of your subscription management page and your payment confirmation.


## Notes
In some cases, it may take up to 24 hours for the subscription status to update after a payment has been made. Additionally, if you have multiple subscriptions or add-ons, ensure that you are checking the correct subscription status. If you are still experiencing issues, our support team may request additional information to investigate and resolve the problem.
