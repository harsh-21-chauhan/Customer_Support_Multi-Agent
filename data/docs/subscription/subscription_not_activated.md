# Subscription not activated

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You have signed up for a subscription, but it is not activated and you are unable to access the paid features of the service. 

## Solution
1. Check your email for a confirmation email from our service, and verify that your account has been successfully created.
2. Ensure that your payment method has been successfully charged, and that the payment has been processed.
3. Log out of the application and log back in to refresh your account status.
4. If you are still unable to access paid features, check your account dashboard for any notifications or alerts regarding your subscription status.
5. If none of the above steps resolve the issue, contact our support team with your account email and a description of the problem, and we will investigate and assist with activating your subscription.


## Notes
If you are using a free trial, note that trial periods are limited and will expire after the specified duration. Additionally, if your payment method is declined or fails to process, your subscription will not be activated. In some cases, it may take a few minutes for the payment to be processed and the subscription to be activated.
