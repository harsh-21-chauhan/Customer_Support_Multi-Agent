# Extend free trial

## Category
subscription

## Problem
You want to extend your free trial period for our SaaS product, but you're not sure how to do it or if it's possible. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account on our website and navigate to the "Subscription" or "Account" section.
2. Click on the "Free Trial" or "Subscription Status" link to view your current trial details.
3. If you are eligible for an extension, you will see an "Extend Trial" button. Click on this button to submit a request for an extension.
4. Our system will review your request and may approve an extension of a limited duration, typically 7-14 days, depending on your usage and other factors.
5. If your request is approved, you will receive an email notification with the new trial end date.


## Notes
Free trial extensions are granted on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed. The availability and duration of extensions may vary depending on your specific plan, usage, and other factors. If you have already used a free trial extension in the past, you may not be eligible for another one. Additionally, some features may be limited or restricted during the extended trial period.
