# Plan features not reflected

## Category
subscription

## Problem
Your current plan features are not reflected in your SaaS account, and you are unable to access the features you expect based on your subscription. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your SaaS account and navigate to the account settings or billing section.
2. Verify that your subscription is active and that the plan details match your expectations.
3. Check for any pending updates or syncing issues by refreshing the page or logging out and back in.
4. If the issue persists, review your email inbox for any notifications regarding plan changes or updates that may have been sent by our system.
5. If you are still unable to access the expected features, submit a support request with a detailed description of the issue, including your account ID and the specific features you are trying to access.


## Notes
Plan features may take a few minutes to propagate after a subscription change. Additionally, some features may require additional setup or configuration after they are enabled. If you have recently upgraded or downgraded your plan, please allow some time for the changes to take effect. In rare cases, caching issues may cause delays in reflecting plan changes; clearing your browser cache may resolve this issue.
