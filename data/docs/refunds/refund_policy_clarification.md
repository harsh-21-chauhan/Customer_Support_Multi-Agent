# Refund policy clarification

## Category
refunds

## Problem
I'm unsure about the refund policy for my subscription and would like to know if I'm eligible for a refund. 

## Solution
1. Check your subscription plan details to see if you're within the refund period, which is typically 30 days from the date of purchase.
2. Review our refund policy terms, which can be found in our terms of service agreement or by contacting our support team.
3. If you're eligible for a refund, submit a refund request through our support portal or by emailing our support team directly.
4. Provide any required information, such as your subscription ID or reason for refund, to facilitate the refund process.
5. Allow 5-10 business days for the refund to be processed and credited back to your original payment method.


## Notes
Refunds are only applicable to the most recent payment and cannot be applied to previous payments or subscription periods. Additionally, refunds may not be available for certain promotional or discounted plans. If you've already utilized a significant portion of the service or have exceeded a certain usage threshold, you may not be eligible for a full refund.
