# Refund on annual plan

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have purchased an annual plan for our SaaS product, but you would like to cancel and receive a refund. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section.
2. Click on the "Cancel Subscription" button next to your annual plan.
3. Confirm that you want to cancel your subscription and request a refund.
4. Our system will automatically process a prorated refund based on the number of days remaining in your annual plan.
5. The refund will be credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.


## Notes
Refunds are only available for annual plans that have been canceled within the first 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, no refunds will be issued. Additionally, any custom or add-on services purchased are non-refundable. If you have any questions or concerns about the refund process, please contact our support team for assistance.
