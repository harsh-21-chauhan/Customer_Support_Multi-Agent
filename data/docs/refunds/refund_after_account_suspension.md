# Refund after account suspension

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have been suspended from using our SaaS platform and would like to know how to obtain a refund for any unused subscription time or prepaid services. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account dashboard, if possible, to access the billing section. If you are unable to log in due to suspension, proceed to step 2.
2. Contact our support team via email or the in-app support chat to request a refund, providing your account name and the reason for the suspension.
3. Our support team will review your account status and verify any unused subscription time or prepaid services eligible for a refund.
4. If eligible, we will process a refund to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
5. You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed, including the amount refunded and any remaining balance.


## Notes
Refunds are only issued for unused subscription time or prepaid services, and may not include setup fees, add-ons, or other non-recurring charges. In cases where a suspension is due to a violation of our terms of service, refunds may be subject to review and approval by our management team. Refunds are typically processed in the original payment currency, but may be subject to exchange rate fluctuations if the refund is issued in a different currency.
