# Refund receipt request

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have received a refund for a subscription or payment, but you have not received a receipt for the refund and would like to request one. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account on our website and navigate to the "Billing" or "Subscriptions" section.
2. Click on the "Refund" or "Payment History" tab to find the specific refund you are looking for.
3. Click on the "Request Receipt" or "View Receipt" button next to the refund, if available.
4. If the button is not available, click on the "Contact Support" link and submit a request for a refund receipt, including the refund date and amount.
5. Our support team will verify the refund and send a receipt to the email address associated with your account.


## Notes
Refund receipts are only available for refunds processed in the last 12 months. If your refund is older than 12 months, we may not be able to provide a receipt. Additionally, refund receipts will only be sent to the email address associated with the account that received the refund.
