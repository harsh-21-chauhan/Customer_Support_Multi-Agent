# Partial refund request

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have requested a cancellation of your subscription, but you have already paid for a period that has not yet been used, and you would like to request a partial refund for the unused portion. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section.
2. Click on the "Refund Request" button and select "Partial Refund" as the reason for your request.
3. Enter the number of unused days or months for which you are requesting a refund.
4. Provide a brief explanation for your refund request, including the date you wish the cancellation to take effect.
5. Submit your refund request for review.
6. Our support team will review your request and process the refund if approved, which may take 3-5 business days.


## Notes
Please note that partial refunds are only available for subscriptions that have been paid in advance, and the amount refunded will be calculated based on the number of unused days or months. Additionally, refunds can only be issued to the original payment method. If you have any promotions or discounts applied to your subscription, the refund amount may be adjusted accordingly. Refund requests must be submitted before the end of the billing cycle to be eligible for a partial refund.
