# Chargeback initiated

## Category
refunds

## Problem
A chargeback has been initiated on your account, and you are unsure of the next steps or how it will affect your subscription. 

## Solution
1. Review your account history to identify the transaction in question.
2. Check your email for any notifications from our system regarding the chargeback.
3. If you believe the chargeback was initiated in error, contact our support team to discuss possible resolution options.
4. If the chargeback is related to a legitimate issue with our service, work with our support team to resolve the underlying problem.
5. Be aware that chargebacks may result in temporary suspension of your account until the issue is resolved.
6. Once the chargeback is resolved, you may need to update your payment method to prevent future disruptions to your service.


## Notes
Chargebacks can take several days to several weeks to resolve, depending on the payment processor and banking institution involved. During this time, your account may be suspended, and you may not be able to access our service. It's essential to communicate with our support team to ensure a smooth resolution. Additionally, repeated chargebacks may lead to account termination, as per our terms of service.
