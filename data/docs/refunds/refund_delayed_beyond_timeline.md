# Refund delayed beyond timeline

## Category
refunds

## Problem
Your refund has not been processed within the expected timeline of 5-7 business days after cancellation of your subscription. 

## Solution
1. Check your email for a refund confirmation email from our support team, which includes the expected refund processing date.
2. If you have not received a refund confirmation email, contact our support team to request an update on the status of your refund.
3. If you have received a refund confirmation email, but the refund has not been processed, please allow for an additional 2-3 business days for the refund to be credited back to your original payment method.
4. If the refund has still not been processed after the additional 2-3 business days, reply to the refund confirmation email with a request for further assistance.


## Notes
Refunds are processed in the order they are received, and processing times may vary due to weekends, holidays, or high volumes of requests. Additionally, some payment methods (such as bank transfers or international transactions) may take longer to process than others (such as credit card refunds). If you have changed your payment method or account information since your subscription was active, please notify our support team to ensure that your refund is processed correctly.
