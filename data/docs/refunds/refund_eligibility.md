# Refund eligibility

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You are unsure if you are eligible for a refund for your SaaS subscription. 

## Solution
1. Review your subscription plan and billing cycle to determine if you are within the refund window.
2. Check your email for the order confirmation email, which includes the refund policy details.
3. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section to see if you have any active subscriptions or pending payments.
4. If you are within the refund window, submit a refund request through the support portal or by contacting our support team directly.
5. Provide any required information, such as your account details and reason for the refund request, to facilitate the refund process.


## Notes
Refund eligibility is determined on a case-by-case basis and may vary depending on the specific subscription plan and billing cycle. Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days, but may take longer in some cases. Please note that refunds may not be available for subscriptions that have been active for more than 30 days or for accounts with outstanding payments.
