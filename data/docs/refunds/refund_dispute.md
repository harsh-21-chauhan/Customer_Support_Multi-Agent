# Refund dispute

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You are disputing a refund that was issued to your account and would like to understand the process for resolving the dispute. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Billing" section.
2. Click on the "Refund History" tab to view a list of all refunds issued to your account.
3. Locate the refund in question and click on the "Dispute" button next to it.
4. Fill out the dispute form with a detailed explanation of why you are disputing the refund, including any relevant order or transaction numbers.
5. Submit the dispute form and wait for our support team to review your case.
6. Our support team will investigate the dispute and may request additional information from you to resolve the issue.
7. Once the dispute is resolved, you will receive an email notification with the outcome and any further instructions.


## Notes
Please note that refund disputes are reviewed on a case-by-case basis and may take several business days to resolve. Additionally, if a refund was issued due to a cancellation or downgrade of your subscription, you may not be eligible to dispute the refund. In cases where a refund was issued in error, we may request that you return the refunded amount before resolving the dispute.
