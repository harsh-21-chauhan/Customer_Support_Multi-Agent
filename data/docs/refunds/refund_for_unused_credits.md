# Refund for unused credits

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have unused credits in your account and would like to request a refund. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Billing" or "Subscription" section.
2. Click on the "Unused Credits" or "Refund Request" option, if available.
3. Fill out the refund request form with the required information, including the number of unused credits you would like to refund.
4. Submit the refund request and wait for our support team to review and process it.
5. Once your refund is approved, the amount will be credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.


## Notes
Refunds for unused credits are subject to our standard refund policy and may be pro-rated based on the amount of time the credits were unused. Additionally, refunds may not be available for credits that were purchased as part of a promotional package or bundle. If you have any questions or concerns about the refund process, please contact our support team for assistance.
