# Refund after downgrade

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have downgraded your subscription plan, but you are not sure how to obtain a refund for the difference in costs between your old and new plans. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section.
2. Click on the "Subscription" tab and verify that your plan has been successfully downgraded.
3. If you are eligible for a refund, a "Refund" button will be displayed next to your new plan details. Click on this button to initiate the refund process.
4. Review the refund amount and confirm that it is correct. The refund amount will be the difference between your old and new plans, prorated for the remaining days in your billing cycle.
5. Once you confirm the refund amount, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to your original payment method.


## Notes
Refunds are only available for downgrades that occur within the same billing cycle. If you have already been billed for the next cycle, you will not be eligible for a refund until the next billing cycle. Additionally, refunds may take longer to process for certain payment methods, such as bank transfers or international transactions.
