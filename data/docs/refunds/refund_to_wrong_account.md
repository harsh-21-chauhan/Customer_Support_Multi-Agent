# Refund to wrong account

## Category
refunds

## Problem
A refund was accidentally issued to the wrong account, and the customer is requesting assistance to correct the mistake and receive the refund in the correct account. 

## Solution
1. Contact our support team via email or chat, providing the order number and a detailed description of the issue.
2. Our support team will verify the refund and account information to confirm the mistake.
3. If the refund was issued to a wrong account, we will cancel the refund and re-process it to the correct account.
4. In some cases, we may require additional information, such as the correct account holder's name and email address, to ensure the refund is issued correctly.
5. Once the refund is re-processed, our support team will notify the customer via email and provide a confirmation of the corrected refund.


## Notes
Refunds can only be re-processed to the original payment method. If the original payment method is no longer valid, the customer may need to contact their bank or payment provider to resolve the issue. Additionally, refunds may take several business days to process, depending on the customer's bank or payment provider.
