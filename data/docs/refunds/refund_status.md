# Refund status

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have requested a refund for your subscription, but you are unsure of the current status of your refund. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Billing" or "Subscriptions" section.
2. Click on the "Refund Requests" or "Order History" tab to view a list of your recent refund requests.
3. Locate the specific refund request you are inquiring about and check the "Status" column.
4. If the status is "Pending", it means that our team is still processing your refund.
5. If the status is "Processed", it means that your refund has been issued and you should receive the funds back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
6. If you are unable to find the refund request or have further questions, contact our support team for assistance.


## Notes
Refund processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider. Additionally, refunds can only be issued to the original payment method used for the subscription. If you have changed your payment method since the original purchase, please contact our support team for further assistance. Refunds are subject to our terms of service and may be delayed or denied if there are any outstanding issues with your account.
