# Duplicate payment refund

## Category
refunds

## Problem
A customer has been charged twice for the same subscription period, resulting in a duplicate payment, and is requesting a refund for the extra charge. 

## Solution
1. Verify the customer's account and payment history to confirm the duplicate payment.
2. Check the payment gateway records to ensure the duplicate payment was not a failed transaction or a pending charge.
3. Process a refund for the duplicate payment amount back to the original payment method.
4. Update the customer's account records to reflect the refund and prevent any further duplicate charges.
5. Notify the customer via email that the refund has been processed and provide a refund reference number.


## Notes
Refunds can take 5-7 business days to appear on the customer's statement, depending on the payment method and bank processing times. In cases where the duplicate payment was made using a different payment method (e.g., credit card vs. PayPal), the refund may need to be processed manually by our support team. Customers should allow sufficient time for the refund to be processed before contacting support to inquire about the status.
