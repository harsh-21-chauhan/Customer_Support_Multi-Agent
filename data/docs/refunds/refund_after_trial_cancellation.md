# Refund after trial cancellation

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have canceled your trial subscription but have not received a refund, and you would like to know how to obtain a refund if you are eligible. 

## Solution
1. Check your email for a cancellation confirmation email from our system, which also includes information on refund eligibility.
2. If you are eligible for a refund, it will be automatically processed within 5-7 business days after your trial cancellation.
3. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during the trial sign-up.
4. If you have not received a refund after 7 business days, please contact our support team with your account details and cancellation date for further assistance.


## Notes
Refunds are only applicable if you have not exceeded the trial period or if there are any specific promotional terms that include a refund. In cases where a payment method is no longer valid (e.g., expired credit card), we may require alternative payment details to process the refund. Please note that refund processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
