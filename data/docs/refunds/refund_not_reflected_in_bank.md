# Refund not reflected in bank

## Category
refunds

## Problem
You have received a refund from our SaaS platform, but the amount has not been reflected in your bank account. 

## Solution
1. Check the refund email sent by our system to confirm that the refund has been processed.
2. Verify that the refund amount is correct and matches the amount you were expecting.
3. Wait for 5-7 business days for the refund to be reflected in your bank account, as this is the typical processing time for banks.
4. If the refund is still not reflected after 7 business days, contact your bank's customer support to inquire about any potential delays or issues.
5. If your bank confirms that they have not received the refund, please contact our support team with your refund reference number and we will investigate further.


## Notes
Refunds are processed immediately on our system, but the time it takes for the amount to be reflected in your bank account depends on your bank's processing time. Additionally, if you have been refunded via a payment method that is no longer active (e.g. expired credit card), the refund may be delayed or require additional steps to resolve. In some cases, refunds may be subject to a small processing fee, which will be deducted from the refund amount.
