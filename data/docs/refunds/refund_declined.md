# Refund declined

## Category
refunds

## Problem
Your refund request has been declined and you are unsure of the reason or the next steps to take. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Refunds" or "Billing" section to view the status of your refund request.
2. Check the decline reason provided in the refund request status message. Common reasons for declined refunds include: the request was made outside of the eligible refund period, the account has an outstanding balance, or the refund has already been processed.
3. If you believe the decline reason is incorrect, contact our support team with your account information and a detailed explanation of your situation.
4. Our support team will review your case and provide a detailed explanation of the decline reason or assist with resubmitting the refund request if necessary.
5. If your refund is re-approved, the refund will be processed within the standard refund processing time frame, which is typically 5-7 business days.


## Notes
Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and the decline reason may vary depending on the specific circumstances of your account. Additionally, refunds can only be issued to the original payment method, and refunds for recurring subscription payments may be prorated based on the time remaining in the billing cycle.
