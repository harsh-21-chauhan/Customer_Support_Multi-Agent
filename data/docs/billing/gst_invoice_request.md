# GST invoice request

## Category
billing

## Problem
You are unable to generate a GST invoice for your subscription payments in our SaaS platform. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section.
2. Click on the "Invoices" tab and select the specific invoice for which you need a GST invoice.
3. Click on the "Request GST Invoice" button next to the invoice date.
4. Fill in the required GST details, including your GST number and business name.
5. Submit the request and wait for our system to generate the GST invoice.
6. Once generated, you can download the GST invoice from the "Invoices" tab.


## Notes
Please note that GST invoices can only be generated for subscriptions paid in INR currency. Additionally, it may take up to 24 hours for the GST invoice to be generated after submitting the request. If you encounter any issues or errors during the process, please contact our support team for assistance.
