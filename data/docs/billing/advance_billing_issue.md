# Advance billing issue

## Category
billing

## Problem
You are experiencing an issue with advance billing, where your account is being charged incorrectly for upcoming periods, resulting in unexpected charges or credits. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the Billing section.
2. Click on the "Billing History" tab to review your previous invoices and identify any discrepancies.
3. Check your subscription plan details to ensure that the billing frequency and amount are correct.
4. If you have recently made changes to your subscription, verify that the changes have been applied correctly.
5. Contact our support team with your account ID and a detailed description of the issue, and we will investigate and adjust your billing accordingly.


## Notes
If you have a custom billing arrangement or have recently upgraded/downgraded your plan, it may take up to 24 hours for the changes to be reflected in your account. Additionally, if you are using a coupon or promo code, please ensure that it is valid and applied correctly to avoid any billing discrepancies. In some cases, advance billing issues may be caused by a temporary system glitch, and our support team may request additional information to resolve the issue.
