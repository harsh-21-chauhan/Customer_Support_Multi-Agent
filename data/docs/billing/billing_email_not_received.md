# Billing email not received

## Category
billing

## Problem
You are expecting to receive a billing email from our system, but it has not arrived in your inbox. 

## Solution
1. Check your spam or junk mail folder to ensure the email was not filtered out.
2. Verify that the email address associated with your account is correct by logging into your account and checking your profile settings.
3. If you have a secondary email address or an email forwarding rule set up, ensure that the billing email is not being redirected to a different inbox.
4. Contact your email provider to see if there are any issues with email delivery or if they are blocking emails from our domain.
5. If none of the above steps resolve the issue, submit a support request and we will investigate further to determine why the billing email was not received.


## Notes
If you are using a custom email domain or have specific email filtering rules in place, it may be necessary to whitelist our email domain to ensure receipt of billing emails. Additionally, if you have recently updated your email address, it may take up to 24 hours for the change to take effect.
