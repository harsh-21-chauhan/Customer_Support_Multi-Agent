# Unauthorized charge

## Category
billing

## Problem
You have noticed an unauthorized charge on your account related to our SaaS service and want to resolve the issue. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section to review your recent transactions.
2. Check the transaction details to confirm that the charge is indeed unauthorized.
3. Contact our support team via email or the in-app support chat, providing the transaction date, amount, and a brief description of the issue.
4. Our support team will investigate the matter and work with you to resolve the issue, which may include refunding the unauthorized charge or adjusting your account balance.
5. If necessary, we will also provide guidance on how to update your account and payment information to prevent similar issues in the future.


## Notes
In some cases, unauthorized charges may be the result of a legitimate transaction that was not recognized by the account holder. It is essential to review your account activity regularly to detect any suspicious transactions. Additionally, if you have recently updated your payment method or added a new user to your account, this may have triggered an automatic payment or invoice. Our support team will work with you to clarify any discrepancies and provide a resolution.
