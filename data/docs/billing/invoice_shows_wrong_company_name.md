# Invoice shows wrong company name

## Category
billing

## Problem
The company name displayed on an invoice is incorrect, which may cause confusion or issues with accounting and record-keeping. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Billing" or "Invoices" section.
2. Click on the specific invoice that shows the incorrect company name.
3. Check the "Billing Information" or "Company Details" section to see if the company name is incorrect there as well.
4. If the company name is incorrect, click on the "Edit" or "Update" button next to the company name field.
5. Update the company name to the correct one and save the changes.
6. Regenerate the invoice to reflect the updated company name.


## Notes
If you have recently updated your company name, it may take some time for the changes to propagate to all invoices. Additionally, if you are using a custom invoice template, you may need to update the template manually to reflect the new company name. In some cases, invoices that have already been sent or paid may not be updated automatically, so you may need to manually update those invoices or contact support for assistance.
