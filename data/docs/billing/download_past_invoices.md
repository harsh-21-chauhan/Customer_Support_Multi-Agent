# Download past invoices

## Category
billing

## Problem
You are unable to download past invoices for your account, and need access to them for record-keeping or expense reporting purposes. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Billing" section.
2. Click on the "Invoices" tab to view a list of all past invoices.
3. Locate the specific invoice you want to download and click on the "Download" button next to it.
4. Select the desired file format (e.g. PDF) and click "OK" to initiate the download.
5. If you are unable to find a specific invoice, use the search function to filter by date range or invoice number.


## Notes
Invoices are only available for download for a period of 2 years from the date of issue. If you need access to invoices older than 2 years, please contact support for assistance. Additionally, invoices can only be downloaded in PDF format, and may not be available for download if your account is inactive or has been cancelled.
