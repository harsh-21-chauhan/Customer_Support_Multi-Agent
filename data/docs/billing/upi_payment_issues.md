# UPI payment issues

## Category
billing

## Problem
You are experiencing issues with making a payment using UPI (Unified Payments Interface) in our application, resulting in failed transactions or error messages. 

## Solution
1. Ensure that your UPI account is active and has sufficient funds to complete the transaction.
2. Verify that your UPI PIN is correct and that you are using the correct UPI ID.
3. Check if there are any pending transactions or holds on your UPI account that may be causing the issue.
4. Try restarting the payment process and confirm that you are using the latest version of our application.
5. If the issue persists, contact your bank's customer support to check for any UPI-related issues on their end.


## Notes
If you have recently changed your UPI PIN or UPI ID, it may take some time to reflect in our system. Additionally, some banks may have specific requirements or restrictions for UPI transactions, so it's best to check with your bank for their policies. In case of multiple failed transactions, you may see temporary holds on your UPI account as a security measure.
