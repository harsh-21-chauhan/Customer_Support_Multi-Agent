# Change payment method

## Category
billing

## Problem
You want to update the payment method associated with your account to change the credit card or switch to a different payment provider. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Billing" section.
2. Click on the "Payment Methods" tab.
3. Click the "Add New Payment Method" button.
4. Enter the new payment method details, such as credit card information or payment provider credentials.
5. Click "Save" to add the new payment method to your account.
6. If you want to remove the old payment method, click the "Delete" button next to the outdated payment method.
7. Confirm that the new payment method is selected as the default payment method for future transactions.


## Notes
If you have an active subscription, the new payment method will be used for the next billing cycle. If you're experiencing issues with adding a new payment method, ensure that your browser is up-to-date and that you're using a supported payment provider. In some cases, you may need to verify your account or payment method through a separate process.
