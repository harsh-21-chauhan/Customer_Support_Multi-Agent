# Tax calculation incorrect

## Category
billing

## Problem
The tax amount calculated on my invoice is incorrect, and I need to ensure the correct tax rate is applied to my subscription. 

## Solution
1. Verify your billing address: Ensure that your billing address is up-to-date and accurate, as tax rates are often determined by location.
2. Check tax settings: Log in to your account and navigate to the Billing section. Click on Tax Settings to confirm that the correct tax rate is selected.
3. Review tax exemptions: If your organization is tax-exempt, ensure that you have uploaded the required documentation and that the exemption is applied to your account.
4. Update tax rates: If you have recently moved to a new location or if tax rates have changed, update your tax settings to reflect the new rates.
5. Contact support: If you have verified the above steps and the tax amount is still incorrect, contact our support team with your account details and a detailed description of the issue.


## Notes
Tax rates and calculations can vary depending on the location and type of subscription. If you are using a custom tax rate, ensure that it is correctly configured. Additionally, tax rates are subject to change, and it is the customer's responsibility to ensure that their tax settings are up-to-date. In cases where the tax amount is incorrect due to a system error, our support team will work with you to resolve the issue and provide a corrected invoice.
