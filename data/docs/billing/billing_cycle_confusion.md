# Billing cycle confusion

## Category
billing

## Problem
You are unsure about how our billing cycle works and when you will be charged for your subscription. 

## Solution
1. Our billing cycle is based on the date you signed up for your subscription.
2. If you signed up for a monthly subscription, you will be charged on the same day of each month.
3. If you signed up for an annual subscription, you will be charged on the same day of each year.
4. You can view your billing cycle details, including your next billing date, by logging into your account and navigating to the "Billing" section.
5. If you need to update your billing information or change your subscription plan, you can do so at any time, but changes will only take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.


## Notes
Please note that if you downgrade or upgrade your subscription plan, you will not receive a prorated refund or charge for the remaining days in your current billing cycle. Additionally, if you cancel your subscription, you will still have access to our service until the end of your current billing cycle, but you will not be charged again after that.
