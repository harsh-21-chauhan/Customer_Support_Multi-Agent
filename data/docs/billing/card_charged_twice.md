# Card charged twice

## Category
billing

## Problem
You noticed that your credit card has been charged twice for the same billing period, resulting in an unexpected duplicate payment. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section to verify the duplicate charge.
2. Check your email for any receipts or invoices related to the duplicate charge.
3. Contact our support team via email or chat, providing the following information: your account name, the date of the duplicate charge, and the amount that was charged.
4. Our support team will investigate the issue and process a refund for the duplicate charge if it was an error on our part.
5. If the duplicate charge was due to a user error (e.g., accidental multiple subscriptions), our support team will guide you through the process of cancelling the duplicate subscription.


## Notes
In some cases, duplicate charges may appear as separate transactions due to differences in billing cycles or payment processing times. If you have recently upgraded or downgraded your plan, this may also cause multiple charges to appear on your statement. Our support team will work with you to resolve the issue and prevent future duplicate charges. 

## Keywords
duplicate charge, double billing, credit card error, billing issue, payment processing, refund request, subscription management
