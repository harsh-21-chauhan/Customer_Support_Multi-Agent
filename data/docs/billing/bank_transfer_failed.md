# Bank transfer failed

## Category
billing

## Problem
Your bank transfer payment for our SaaS service has failed, and you are unsure of the cause or how to resolve the issue. 

## Solution
1. Check your bank account to ensure that the transfer was initiated and that there are sufficient funds to cover the payment.
2. Verify that the bank transfer details, including the recipient's name, account number, and routing number, were entered correctly.
3. Confirm that your bank does not have any restrictions or blocks on international transactions, if applicable.
4. Contact your bank to see if they have any information about the failed transfer, such as a reference number or error code.
5. If the issue persists, try re-initiating the bank transfer or consider using an alternative payment method, such as a credit card.


## Notes
Bank transfers can take several days to process, and failures may occur due to various reasons, including insufficient funds, incorrect account information, or bank-specific issues. If you are still experiencing issues, please contact our support team for further assistance. Additionally, please note that some banks may charge fees for failed or returned transactions.
