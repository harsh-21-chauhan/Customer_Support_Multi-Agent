# Billing currency mismatch

## Category
billing

## Problem
You are being charged in a different currency than the one you selected during sign-up, resulting in unexpected exchange rates and fees. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the Billing section.
2. Click on the "Edit Billing Information" button.
3. Verify that the selected currency matches your preferred currency.
4. If the currency is incorrect, click on the "Change Currency" button and select the correct currency from the dropdown list.
5. Review and confirm the changes to ensure the correct currency is applied to your account.


## Notes
Please note that changing the billing currency may affect the pricing of your subscription, as prices may vary depending on the currency. Additionally, if you have already been charged in the incorrect currency, you may need to contact our support team to request a refund or adjustment. It's also important to be aware that some payment methods may not support all available currencies, which could result in an error during the payment process.
