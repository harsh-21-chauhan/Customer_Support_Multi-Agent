# Payment stuck in pending

## Category
billing

## Problem
Your payment is stuck in a pending state and you are unable to access the paid features of our service. 

## Solution
1. Check your email for any payment confirmation emails from our system, as sometimes payments can take a few hours to process.
2. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section to check the status of your payment.
3. If the payment is still pending after 24 hours, try restarting the payment process by clicking on the "Retry Payment" button.
4. If the issue persists, contact our support team with your account ID and the date of the pending payment, and we will investigate the issue further.
5. In some cases, we may request additional information, such as a screenshot of the payment error or your bank statement, to help resolve the issue.


## Notes
Payments can be stuck in a pending state due to various reasons, including bank processing delays, incorrect payment information, or issues with our payment gateway. If you have recently changed your billing address or payment method, please ensure that the information is up-to-date and accurate. Additionally, some payment methods, such as e-checks, may take longer to process than credit card payments.
