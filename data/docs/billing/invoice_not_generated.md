# Invoice not generated

## Category
billing

## Problem
You have completed a payment cycle, but an invoice for your subscription has not been generated in our system. 

## Solution
1. Check your account dashboard to ensure that your payment has been successfully processed.
2. Verify that your billing information, including your email address and company details, is up-to-date and accurate.
3. Navigate to the "Invoices" section of your account dashboard and click on "Generate Invoice" to manually trigger the invoice creation process.
4. If the invoice is still not generated, check your email spam folder to ensure that the invoice email was not mistakenly flagged as spam.
5. If none of the above steps resolve the issue, contact our support team with your account details and a description of the problem for further assistance.


## Notes
Invoices are typically generated automatically within 24 hours of a successful payment. However, in some cases, manual intervention may be required. Additionally, if your account is set up for automatic invoice emailing, please ensure that your email address is correctly configured to receive emails from our system. If you have recently updated your billing information, please allow up to 24 hours for the changes to take effect.
