# Update billing address

## Category
billing

## Problem
You need to update the billing address associated with your account, but you're not sure how to do it. 

## Solution
1. Log in to your account and navigate to the "Billing" or "Account Settings" section.
2. Click on the "Edit Billing Information" or "Update Billing Address" button.
3. Enter your new billing address in the provided fields, including street address, city, state, and zip code.
4. Review your updated billing address for accuracy and click "Save Changes" to confirm.
5. Verify that your billing address has been updated successfully by checking the "Billing" or "Account Settings" section.


## Notes
If you have multiple accounts or subscriptions, you may need to update the billing address for each one separately. Additionally, updating your billing address may not affect any pending or scheduled payments, so you should review your payment schedule to ensure that it reflects the changes. If you encounter any issues while updating your billing address, you can contact our support team for assistance.
