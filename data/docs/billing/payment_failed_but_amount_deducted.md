# Payment failed but amount deducted

## Category
billing

## Problem
Your payment for our SaaS service has failed, but the amount has been deducted from your account. 

## Solution
1. Check your email for a payment failure notification from our system, which may include details about the reason for the failure.
2. Log in to your account and navigate to the billing section to verify the payment status.
3. If the payment is marked as failed, try to initiate the payment again using the same payment method.
4. If the issue persists, contact your bank or payment provider to confirm that the amount was deducted and to request their assistance in resolving the issue.
5. If the bank confirms that the amount was deducted, please contact our support team with the transaction details, including the date, amount, and payment method used, so we can investigate and provide a refund or adjust your account balance accordingly.


## Notes
In some cases, payment failures may occur due to temporary issues with the payment gateway or your bank's system. If you are using a credit or debit card, please ensure that your card is valid, has sufficient funds, and that your card issuer allows international transactions (if applicable). Additionally, some banks may take a few days to release the deducted amount back to your account.
